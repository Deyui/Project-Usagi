This year's nationwide Animate Valentine event!

2020.2.4
On the 14th February 2020 we will be holding a 「Is the Order a Rabbit??」Valentine event for all of Animate shops in the country!

On that day you will be able to receive a special Valentine card on a first-come first-serve basis. You will also be able to receive new ReBirth PR Cards (randomly), which all have QR Codes at the back which you can scan and listen to their newly recorded voices!

The password for this year is 「Café Late！Caffé Mocha！Cappuccino！」. Please make sure to say it in a loud voice as if trained by Rize!.

So please make sure to visit Animate on the upcoming 14th February!

【Information regarding the Valentine Card】
Event date: 14th February 2020
Location: All Animate Stores in the country
(With the exception of Animate Online)


※ Please be aware that the distribution ends as soon as they're gone.

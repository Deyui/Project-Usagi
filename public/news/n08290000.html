Nationwide New Year's Card Distribution Campaign at Animate and Gamers!

2019.12.27
With the campaign you'll be able to receive a New Year's Card in any Animate or Gamers shop in the whole country!!!

Starting on the 1st January a special New Year's Card will be gifted there!
So please make sure to visit your most convenient store!

Duration: 1st January 2020 (Wednesday)
※ Distribution will end as soon as when no cards are left.

Details: During the period, every customer who visits either an Animate or Gamers shop in Japan will be able to receive a special New Year's Card for every transaction!
※ Stores that are closed on the 1st January will distribute it on the 2nd
※ Cancellations or postponements are subject to change depending on various circumstances
※ Campaign is not eligble for Animate Online and Gamers Online
